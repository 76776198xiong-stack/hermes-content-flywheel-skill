## Onboarding Questions

Ask at most five at once:

1. Who are you trying to help right now?
2. What do they usually misunderstand?
3. What do you see that others in your field do not see?
4. What product, service, or community should the content eventually support?
5. Paste one thing you wrote that sounds like you.

If the user is overwhelmed, skip onboarding and produce one content package from their current thought.
